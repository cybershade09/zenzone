How do you know whether you have anxiety?

Symptoms of Anxiety

If Anxiety is left unaddressed, symptoms of anxiety can impact work performance, relationships, and daily activities, creating a cycle of worry, stress, and exhaustion. Some key symptoms include

1. Persistent Negative Thoughts

2. Heart Palptiations and/or difficulty breathing

3. A sense of helplessness



Causes of Anxiety

Anxiety can arise from a combination of biological, environmental, and lifestyle factors. Here some common causes

1. Family History

- Having a family history of anxiety or other mental health conditions can increase the likelihood of developing anxiety

2. Social Media

- Excessive use of social media can lead to constant comparison, feelings of inadequacy, and a fear of missing out (FOMO), all of which contribute to anxiety.

3. Traumatic Life Experiences

- Experiencing trauma such as physical, emotional, or sexual abuse—can trigger anxiety disorders, including Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD).

4. Substance Abuse

- Excessive consumption of caffeine, alcohol, or tobacco has been linked to a higher risk of developing anxiety disorders.
