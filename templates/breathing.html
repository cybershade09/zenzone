4-7-8 breathing technique

The 4-7-8 breathing technique is a simple breathing exercise designed to promote relaxation and reduce stress. It was popularized by Dr. Andrew Weil and is based on ancient yogic breathing practices (pranayama).


Breathe in 1 2 3 4 Hold in 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 Breathe Out 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
