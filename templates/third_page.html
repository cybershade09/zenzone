Struggling With Anxiety? Try This Breathing Exercise! (4-7-8 Breathing Technique)

1. Exhale Completely

2. Close your lips and inhale silently through your nose while counting to 4 seconds

3. Hold your breath for 7 seconds

4. Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 seconds, making the whooshing sound again
